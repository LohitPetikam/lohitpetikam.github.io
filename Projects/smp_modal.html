Spectral Mesh Processing in Blender

Spectral mesh processing plugin for Blender. Github: https://github.com/LohitPetikam/SpectralMesh

Basic implementation of Spectral mesh processing [Bruno LÃ©vy and Hao (Richard) Zhang. 2010 ACM SIGGRAPH Courses]

Spectral mesh processing is a way of decomposing a 3D surface mesh into a series of harmonics with different frequencies. Manipulating the harmonics gives interesting transformations to the mesh.

Here, the font is animated as a 3D mesh with the audio spectrum driving the manifold harmonics. Note that this is more than simple scaling based on audio volume. The font becomes spikier or rounder as the sounds and instruments become dull and sharp respectively.
